Dear Diva Apriliyana

From,kodomo

Thank you for everything—for the wonderful three months we spent together and for the gift you gave me. I have read your letter from beginning to end. I truly hope this won't be the last time we meet or stay in touch. To me, you are more than just a friend—more than even a "good friend." I hope that someday, you'll still remember me. Perhaps by now you've realized that I was only pretending not to know anything. The truth is, I heard everything you talked about with Khanza, Kame, Filzah, Kak Imad, and the others at JILC after 2:00 PM that day. But I deliberately chose not to respond to the hints you gave me because I wanted you to succeed through the hard work you've fought for, and I didn't want to ruin any of it. Thank you for everything. Meeting you is a memory I will never forget. To me, you are truly one of a kind, and I can say that with all my heart. Please know that everything I ever gave you came from a place of sincerity. I gave it because I was genuinely happy to know you and to have had the chance to meet you. Once again, thank you for everything. I promise I will never forget these memories. Take good care of yourself in your new place. I hope you enjoy meeting new people, and may the Island of the Gods, Bali, always watch over and protect you. Don't forget to take your medication, and don't forget to bring your Hot In Cream. It's truly a lifesaver whenever your gout flares up. Thank you once again. And lastly, remember my name. Abiy—and I will always be Abiy. Makassar, July 4, 2026
